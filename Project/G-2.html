Section G-2: Identifying Conflicts of Interest

Identifying Conflicts of Interest

In this section, you will answer a series of questions to help you, determine if the activitiy you are considering could create a conflict of interest

For the purpose of this tool, the following definintions are used:

    Interests: An Interest is a concern or commitment associated with a social relationship or practice
      Interest include both self-orientated concerns (where one stands to benefit or lose) and other-orientated commitments (where one has a responsibility to another but does not necesarily stand to gain or lose).

Note that in each of these questions you should consider whether any impact is theoretically possible, even if you believe that you would be able to prevent it happening, or manage it effectively.
